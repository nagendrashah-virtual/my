Privacy

Protecting the data you share with us is our top priority. Read our Privacy Policy to understand what information we collect about you and why, how and when we share with our partners, and how we manage the data that you enter in our products.

Availability

Our online systems are critical for your work, therefore we take numerous steps and measures to monitor and maintain their availability and accessibility of your data. Our real-time services availability status page provides immediate overview of all services' uptime with history.

Security

We do our best to make sure our software is free of security vulnerabilities. Reality though is that it's not always the case. That is why we are very much eager and grateful to hear about any issues you find. Please report these directly to our issue tracker (setting issue type to 'Security Problem') or via email. You can find our PGP key on most public key servers.
